<dl>
<dt>Armed conflicts and attacks</dt>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Syrian_Civil_War" title="Syrian Civil War">Syrian Civil War</a>
<ul>
<li>A truce is in effect, as of midnight. <a href="/wiki/Russian_Air_Force" title="Russian Air Force">Russian Air Force</a> attacks on opposition positions were reported to have been intensified before the truce took effect. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-35674908">(BBC)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/War_in_Afghanistan_(2015%E2%80%93present)" title="War in Afghanistan (2015–present)">War in Afghanistan (2015–present)</a>
<ul>
<li>A <a href="/wiki/Suicide_bomber" class="mw-redirect" title="Suicide bomber">suicide bomber</a> kills at least 26 and injures nearly 50 in <a href="/wiki/Afghanistan" title="Afghanistan">Afghanistan</a>'s eastern <a href="/wiki/Kunar_Province" title="Kunar Province">Kunar Province</a> near the border with <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistan</a>. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.news.com.au/world/breaking-news/suicide-bomber-kills-11-at-afghan-compound/news-story/53af4814d4fe82818f0e0601e77379ba?from=public_rss">(AAP via News Limited)</a> <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.nytimes.com/2016/02/28/world/asia/suicide-bombing-in-eastern-afghanistan-kills-at-least-12.html?_r=0">(NY Times)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yemeni_Civil_War_(2015%E2%80%93present)" title="Yemeni Civil War (2015–present)">Yemeni Civil War (2015–present)</a>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Saudi_Arabian-led_intervention_in_Yemen" title="Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen">Saudi Arabian-led intervention in Yemen</a>
<ul>
<li>At least 30 people are killed in an airstrike on a market near the <a href="/wiki/Yemen" title="Yemen">Yemeni</a> capital <a href="/wiki/Sana%27a" title="Sana'a">Sana'a</a>. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-35678326">(BBC)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dt>Law and crime</dt>
</dl>
<ul>
<li>Three people are stabbed, including one person critically wounded, and several people are arrested after a <a href="/wiki/Ku_Klux_Klan" title="Ku Klux Klan">Ku Klux Klan</a> (KKK) rally in <a href="/wiki/Anaheim,_California" title="Anaheim, California">Anaheim, California</a> turns violent. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.latimes.com/local/lanow/la-me-ln-klan-rally-in-anaheim-erupts-in-violence-one-man-stabbed-20160227-story.html">(Los Angeles Times)</a></li>
</ul>
<dl>
<dt>Politics and elections</dt>
</dl>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/United_States_presidential_election,_2016" title="United States presidential election, 2016">United States presidential election, 2016</a>
<ul>
<li>Voters in <a href="/wiki/South_Carolina" title="South Carolina">South Carolina</a> go to the polls for the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Party_(United_States)" title="Democratic Party (United States)">Democratic Party</a> primary. <a href="/wiki/Hillary_Clinton" title="Hillary Clinton">Hillary Clinton</a> won. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://abcnews.go.com/Politics/live-south-carolina-democratic-primary-exit-poll-analysis/story?id=37241467">(ABC)</a> <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.al.com/news/index.ssf/2016/02/south_carolina_democratic_prim.html">(AL.com)</a> <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.theguardian.com/us-news/live/2016/feb/27/south-carolina-democratic-primary-hillary-clinton-bernie-sanders-campaign-live">(<i>The Guardian</i>)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Irish_general_election,_2016" title="Irish general election, 2016">Irish general election, 2016</a>
<ul>
<li>Counting begins of the votes cast yesterday with exit polls indicating that the existing coalition will not gain enough votes to govern without reaching a deal with other parties. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-35674465">(BBC)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Elections_in_Iran" title="Elections in Iran">Elections in Iran</a>
<ul>
<li>Counting of votes begins in <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a> after polling hours in yesterday's <a href="/wiki/Iranian_legislative_election,_2016" title="Iranian legislative election, 2016">election</a> were extended by six hours due to a heavy turnout. Reports indicate that <a href="/wiki/Iranian_Reformists" title="Iranian Reformists">reformist</a> candidates are doing well in the early count. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.swissinfo.ch/eng/iran-starts-election-count-to-set-post-sanctions-power-balance/41984890">(Reuters via Swiss, info)</a>, <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://bigstory.ap.org/article/64a1faf3db0247fb9e03d5cee27eae12/iran-reformists-performing-well-early-election-results">(AP)</a></li>
<li>Preliminary results shows that <a href="/wiki/Pervasive_Coalition_of_Reformists" title="Pervasive Coalition of Reformists">Pervasive Coalition of Reformists</a> has won a majority in <a href="/wiki/Tehran" title="Tehran">Tehran</a> district and some other big cities, as <a href="/wiki/Principlists_Coalition" class="mw-redirect" title="Principlists Coalition">Principlists Coalition</a> won seats in smaller cities. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.reuters.com/article/us-iran-election-vote-idUSKCN0VZ0E7">(Reuters)</a></li>
<li>Early <a href="/wiki/Assembly_of_Experts" title="Assembly of Experts">Assembly of Experts</a> <a href="/wiki/Iranian_Assembly_of_Experts_election,_2016" title="Iranian Assembly of Experts election, 2016">results</a> show that <a href="/wiki/Iranian_Principlists" title="Iranian Principlists">Principlists</a> won the majority, as Reformists won 14 out of 16 seats in <a href="/wiki/Tehran" title="Tehran">Tehran</a>, although some of them also had the support of conservatives. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://news.yahoo.com/irans-rouhani-top-ally-rafsanjani-lead-assembly-experts-135559253.html">(Reuters via Yahoo)</a></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Thousands, including some from <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, protest in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, against the renewal of the <a href="/wiki/UK_Trident_programme" class="mw-redirect" title="UK Trident programme">United Kingdom's Trident nuclear deterrent system</a>, the largest anti-nuclear rally since 1983. <a href="/wiki/Leader_of_the_Labour_Party_(UK)" title="Leader of the Labour Party (UK)">Leader of the Labour Party</a> <a href="/wiki/Jeremy_Corbyn" title="Jeremy Corbyn">Jeremy Corbyn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scottish_National_Party" title="Scottish National Party">Scottish National Party</a> leader and <a href="/wiki/First_Minister_of_Scotland" title="First Minister of Scotland">First Minister of Scotland</a> <a href="/wiki/Nicola_Sturgeon" title="Nicola Sturgeon">Nicola Sturgeon</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Welsh</a> <a href="/wiki/Plaid_Cymru" title="Plaid Cymru">Plaid Cymru Party</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Green_Party_of_England_and_Wales" title="Green Party of England and Wales">Green Party</a> participated in the march. A <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="Parliament of the United Kingdom">parliamentary</a> vote on renewing Trident is expected this year. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.ibtimes.com/rally-london-against-trident-nuclear-deterrent-system-draws-thousands-2326327">(<i>International Business Times</i>)</a> <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.theguardian.com/world/2016/feb/27/cnd-rally-anti-nuclear-demonstration-trident-london">(<i>The Guardian</i>)</a></li>
<li>A large rally, with estimated counts ranging from 7,500 to 100,000, honors former <a href="/wiki/Deputy_Chairman_of_the_Government_of_the_Russian_Federation" class="mw-redirect" title="Deputy Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation">deputy prime minister</a> and <a href="/wiki/Russian_opposition" class="mw-redirect" title="Russian opposition">opposition</a> leader <a href="/wiki/Boris_Nemtsov" title="Boris Nemtsov">Boris Nemtsov</a> on the first anniversary of his <a href="/wiki/Assassination_of_Boris_Nemtsov" title="Assassination of Boris Nemtsov">assassination</a> near the <a href="/wiki/Kremlin" title="Kremlin">Kremlin</a>. <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.reuters.com/article/us-russia-nemtsov-idUSKCN0W00M4">(Reuters)</a></li>
</ul>
</td>
